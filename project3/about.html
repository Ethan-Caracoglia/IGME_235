Project 3 Proposal

Game: Surrounded

What is it?

This game will feature a enemies that come in increasingly numerous waves. The player will
be given different types of melee and ranged weapons to battle these enemies with. How long
will they be able to survive?

Details/Genre:

  • Rougelike
  • Shoot 'em Up
  • Top Down
  • Platform: Desktop-Browser

Story

In this game you are subjected to a punishment similar to Sisyphus in hell. Your boulder
will be the relentless crowd of enemies that have no end.

Style

The style of this game is intended to be extremely simple as the focus is going to be on
using stick figures and hand drawn objects in a sketch-like art style. Color will be used to denote
the players and different types of enemies.

Gameplay

The gameplay will be pretty standard for a shoot 'em up type of game with a top down view.
It will feature at least 3 differnt types of weapons and enemies which vary between being
ranged and melee based. With each wave the number and strength of enemies will grow up until
a point where the enemies plateau and only their numbers increase.

Controls

The player will be able to move using the "WASD" keys as well as the arrow keys. They will
attack in whatever direction they are facing using "J" or "SPACEBAR".
